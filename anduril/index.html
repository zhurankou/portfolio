01 // About

I design systems that make complex data accessible. With a focus on B2B product design, I work at the intersection of information architecture, data visualization, and systematic design thinking.

From medical device dashboards to enterprise cloud interfaces, I bring structure and clarity to products that serve millions of users. Every pixel is a data point. Every interaction is measurable.

My approach is rooted in research-driven methodology, systematic component architecture, and a relentless focus on reducing cognitive load for end users working with dense, critical information.
